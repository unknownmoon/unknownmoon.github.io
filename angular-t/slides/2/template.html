Use expression inside {{ }} or with ng-bind series directives
This is the most common used scenario, since simple data-binding is also Angular expression.


			
Use expression with directives citing string as their inputs, such as ng-include

			

				
 Bare in mind that the syntax of ng-include is ng-include="{string}", hence the result of the expression should be string.

				
    
					
  •  <div ng-include="path/to/template/template.html" ></div>
    • 
					
  •  <div ng-include=" 'path/to/template/template.html' " ></div>
    •
